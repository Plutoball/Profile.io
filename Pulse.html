01. Research

Heuristic evaluation identifies usability issues, ensuring alignment with best practices. Understanding business goals and value propositions provides context for user needs and design decisions. These insights shape a research-driven approach to creating effective UX/UI solutions.

02. Ideation

Through site comparison, heuristic evaluation, and scenario testing, we uncover usability challenges and design inefficiencies. Analyzing patterns reveals obstacles that hinder user experience and engagement. These insights guide ideation, ensuring solutions align with user needs and improve effectiveness.

03. Design

We sketch around HMW (How Might We) questions to uncover solutions, then create low-fidelity wireframes to shape ideas. Prioritizing easy-to-implement components ensures usability, while complex, blue-sky elements push boundaries and inspire innovation. This approach balances practicality with creativity, leading to impactful results.
