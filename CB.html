Connected Banking

The task of this design project is to make a dashboard that combines the most accessed feature from both the ABSA online and Connected banking platforms, to create a single platform that is user-friendly, visually appealing, and provides a seamless user experience. The goal is to simplify the banking process and provide the customer with a simple and relevant digital service.

Challenge

The main challenge was to create a user-friendly interface that effectively improves the personal banking experience by integrating the most accessed features from both the ABSA online and Connected Banking platforms.

Solution

The solution involved designing a clean and intuitive interface that combines the most frequently used features from both platforms. The design process included user research, wireframing, and prototyping to ensure the final product meets user needs and expectations.

"Envisioning a simple digital banking interface for personal banking customers."

We started with customer interviews to assess their experience with the current dashboard. Later, we expanded our research to compare both dashboards and their analytics. After brief evaluations, we analyzed the most usable and frequently accessed features across user personas. Finally, we created wireframes and screens based on the findings.

01. Research

Our research began with a detailed comparison of the two dashboards, aiming to identify shared features, design similarities, and usage patterns. We examined functionality, user engagement, and performance metrics to understand how each dashboard supported customer interactions. To gain deeper insights, we then analyzed existing personas, exploring customer demographics, behaviors, and expectations. This helped us understand who our users are, what they value, and how they interact with the dashboard, ensuring our findings aligned with their needs and preferences.

02. Ideation

Ideation was a collaborative effort, where we brainstormed and sketched concepts informed by our research findings. Insights into customers' understanding of devices and their dashboard usage patterns guided decisions on the new layout and functionality. Our focus was on designing an intuitive, user-friendly interface to enhance the overall experience. Ultimately, the goal was to streamline the banking process and provide a seamless, relevant digital service tailored to customer needs.

03. Design

We prioritized key design principles based on insights into customer needs and preferences, ensuring an intuitive content layout and feature placement. Throughout the design process, we refined the visuals by incorporating brand colors and using clear, self-explanatory graphics to enhance usability and engagement.
