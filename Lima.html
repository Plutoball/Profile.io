Lima

Lima is a farm maintenance digital product that aims to help subsistence farmers produce sustainably - using IBM's AI technologies.

2023
UX/UI Designer
Figma . Miro . Slack . Google Meets . IBM Cloud
Website

Challenge

The main challenge was to help farmers manage their farm, prepare for climate risks and grow sustainably with the aid of IBM's AI technologies - packaged in a user-friendly digital product.

Solution

The solution involved designing and creating a digital product that helps farmers get access to intelligently processed information about the current state of their farm, receive actionable insights and recommendations for prevent loss due to climate change and, tools and methods to innovate their farming activities for sustainable farming.

"Ensuring sustainable subsistence farming."

We conducted our research by carefully drafting and following a structured plan. Since most of our team are neither professional farmers nor horticultural enthusiasts, we relied extensively on desktop research to gain a broad understanding of the topic. Based on our findings and initial assumptions, we developed a prototype to test our ideas and initiate discussions with local subsistence farmers.

01. Research

The research phase involved gathering findings through site visits for observation and interview. We believed that understanding the farmers' needs and challenges firsthand would provide valuable insights, as opposed to relying solely on secondary research.

02. Ideation

To get around finding the an apporiate solution, we created a prototype that would allow us to test our ideas and initiate discussions with local subsistence farmers. This prototype was a culmination of ideas we thought would be beneficial to the farmers, and it served as a starting point for further refinement based on user feedback.

03. Design

The concpet of an intelligently processed information about the current state of their farm, receiving actionable insights and recommendations for preventing loss due to climate change, and providing tools and methods to innovate their farming activities for sustainable farming was the main focus of the design phase. This would be the outcome of packangeing IBM Watson resources into a user-friendly digital product.
