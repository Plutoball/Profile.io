Colab

Making space technologies more accessible through the development of an open source microsatellite platform and community.

UX/UI Designer
Figma . Vectary 3D . Similar Web . MS Teams
Website

Challenge

The main challenge was to create a user-friendly interface and content for the resources page, to improve Kispe's OSSAT program website's enagegment.

Solution

The solution involved designing a Colab comprehensive portal that provides users with easy access to information about the OSSAT platform, including guides, informative interactive content, and community support. The design focused on clarity, accessibility, and user engagement to foster a collaborative environment.

"Creating a virtual collaborative experiance."

We ran a comparative analysis of the OSSAT website to access the utility of its content, easy of navigation and overall visual quality. After comparing portal website features and capabilities we created and idea to make Colab an interactive extension of the OSSAT membership website.

01. Research

The research phase involved gathering insights into user needs vs unique offering and audience. This included user interviews, benchmarking and competitive analysis to inform the design direction.

02. Ideation

We moved into the ideation phase, brainstorming innovative ways to meet the needs of KISPE's contributors. After identifying promising ideas, we developed prototypes to test the effectiveness of these solutions. The prototypes helped us visualize the potential outcome and allowed for adjustments based on user feedback. Our focus was to create a user-friendly platform that would make the complex terminology and concepts of the open-source satellite program more accessible.

03. Design

The design process involved creating wireframes, user flows, and high-fidelity mockups to visualize the final product. We ensured that the design was not only visually appealing but also functional and user-friendly. We agreed to name our concept Colab because it would be easy for the user to understand what the web application is for - instantly. The visual treatment of Colab stems from the existing OSSAT corporate identity, keeping it consistent and looking trustworthy.

Conclusion

The development and implementation of the Colab program can significantly improve the collaborative experience on the KISPE's open sources satellite program website. It will not only make the complex content more comprehensible but also fostered a unified community of contributors. As a result, KISPE has been encouraged to secure necessary funding for the development of this web application, demonstrating the effectiveness of our solution.
