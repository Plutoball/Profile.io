Insurance Hub

ABSA short term insurance products team experiment to improve the digital customer experience, in an attempt to investigate and find solution to mitigate drop of rates.

2022
UI Designer
Figma
Website

Challenge

The main challenge was to find out how to improve customer engagements with insurance products on ABSA's digital platforms.

Solution

The solution involved creating a comprehensive digital insurance hub that provides users with easy access to insurance products, information, and services.

"Improving short term digital insurance experiences."

Our solution is a result of a vigorous UCD process where a non-branded prototype was testd by users in a controlled environment. The feedback received was used to inform the design decisions, ensuring that the final product meets user needs and expectations.

01. Research

Using the app as a starting point, we wanted to test two high level journey concepts and get insights on the customer expectations or each journey (manage and quote). We wanted to understand customer pain points for each journey.

02. Ideation

The Ideation phase focused on brainstorming and generating ideas based on the research findings. This involved sketching concepts, creating wireframes,developing user flows to visualize the user journey, and testing initial design concepts with users to gather feedback.

03. Design

Fed by the insights gathered from the research and ideation phases, the design phase focused on creating a comprehensive digital insurance hub.

Conclusion

By putting a sample product in the customers hand we were able to compare our initial assumptions to their needs , expectations, understanding and preferences. This allowed us to improve on a long term concept and design a solution that would enable customers comprehensive and full support when using ABSA's insurance products. We worked well as a team and handed off a clear project.
